How refunds work

Because Paddle is the Merchant of Record, refunds are handled by Paddle and governed by the Paddle Refund Policy. In summary:

  • Buyers in the EU/EEA, UK, Switzerland, Turkey and Israel have a 14-day statutory right to withdraw; buyers in South Korea, Brazil, Canada and China have a 7-day right; and buyers in Singapore have a 5-day right.
  • Paddle may also issue refunds on a discretionary basis within 14 days of the transaction date — for example, where there is evidence of a material technical or product defect.
  • Refunds are not issued where there is evidence of fraud, refund abuse or other manipulative behaviour.

How to request a refund

Use the “View receipt” or “Manage subscription” link in your Paddle order-confirmation email, or go to paddle.net and choose “Request refund.” You can also write to us at support@dai3d.com and we will help you through the process.
